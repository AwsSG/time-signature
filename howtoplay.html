How To Play

What Are Time Signatures?

A time signature tells you how music is to be counted. In essence how many beats or "seconds" in a bar. They are always shown as a "fraction" such as 4/4, 3/4 or 6/8. The top number tells you how many beats to count while the bottom number tells you what note to count.

Most Common Time Signatures

The most common time signatures are signatures over 4 such as 4/4 and 3/4. Everybody is familiar with a 4/4 and 3/4 beat. 4/4 is used in pretty much every pop song ever written and 3/4 is a standard waltz. We've all heard one at a wedding!
